DiviFizz: A Coding Challenge

DiviFizz is a coding challenge that uses JavaScript to enable you to enter two numbers, a starting number and an ending number. The input of these number will allow you to see numbers that are divisible by 3, 5, and 15.

Instructions

Enter a starting number and an ending number. Press the button to see the numbers in that range appear. The appearance of numbers will change by color and by changing into text if they are divisible by 3, 5, and 15.
